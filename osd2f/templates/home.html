Why donate data?


We've all heard stories about how social media attempts to influence us.

We count ourselves lucky: it does not really happen to us. Or does it?

Help us find out how prevelant fake news, misinformation, sleeper-advertisement and negative content actually is!


The OSD2F project aims to study what goes on on social media

We ask you to donate what happens on your timeline, so that we may learn.

Donations can be stripped of personal information and processed so that we only see what goes on on average

By donating, you help us uncover malicous actors, spreading misinformation amongst your friends, track advertisers trying to take advantage of you and help make social media a better place.

This project is part of the University of Amsterdam

Our team is dedicated to preserve your privacy, while guarding society from malicous intent online.

To do so, we are combining many disciplines of social and computer science.

But we need you, to donate your data, in order for us to do our job.
