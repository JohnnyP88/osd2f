Guarding your privacy


Studying your social media feed does not mean studying you.
We respect your privacy and do whatever it takes to guarantee that data you donate is handled properly.


These are our three assurances:



1. We filter only the information we need for our research

Social media know much about you, but not everything is necessary for our research.

We ask for fields such as the titles and body of posts, but not what you 'liked' or 'shared'.

In addition, we will try to remove any identifying fields, such as those containing your name.

2. Control over what you upload

Before uploading your data, you can inspect what you will send within your browser.

Until this point, no data will be transfered and you can simply close your browser to stop your donation

It's not 'all or nothing', instead you select which records and which information is send.
