Step 1

Enroll in our study.
You will receive an invitation email to take part in a survey that relates to this study. At the end of survey, there will be a link guiding you to submit your data.

IMAGE OF SIGNUP GOES HERE

Step 2

Export data from the social media platform.
When following the survey link, you will be instructed to download an export of your data from a social media platform. You are entitled to your own data based on the "General Data Protection Regulation" (GDPR) laws in the EU.

IMAGE OF EXPORT SCREEN GOES HERE

Step 3

Load your data into the data donation tool in your browser
You do not need to install anything. Just select the file you downloaded from the social media platform and click "load". The data will not be send to us yet. Instead you can manually inspect and filter the data as you require.

IMAGE OF FILE SELECTION/FILTER GOES HERE

Step 4

Done checking the data? Donate!
By clicking on "Upload", you will send the filtered data to the OSD2F platform. Thank you!
